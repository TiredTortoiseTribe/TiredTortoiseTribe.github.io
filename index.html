Neocense

Neocense is a story-driven puzzle adventure game set in a fantasy steampunk world in which you solve mysteries,
fight robots, and explore colourful tiered cities! You play as Amori, a young engineer excited to learn more about the world around her.
As you progress through the different tiers of the world, you learn more about Amoris story and the world she lives in.
There is more to discover than meets the eye.....
